Privacy Policy

Offline Holy Bible KJV · Effective July 22, 2026

Offline Holy Bible KJV does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data itself. Everything you do in the app — reading history, bookmarks, reminder settings — stays on your device. The app does display ads served by Google AdMob, described below.

Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, and the app itself does not transmit over the internet, any of the following:

  • Your name, email, or any account information — the app has no accounts
  • Analytics or usage tracking
  • Location data
  • Contacts, photos, or any other device data

The Bible text, reading progress, bookmarks, and offline commentary all work fully offline, with no internet connection required. Internet access is used only to request and display the ads described below when a connection is available.

Data Stored On Your Device

The app stores the following data locally, only on your device:

  • Reading progress — which chapters you've marked as read
  • Bookmarks — verses you've saved
  • Last read location — so "Continue Reading" can pick up where you left off
  • Reminder settings — whether a daily reading reminder is enabled and at what time

None of this data ever leaves your device. Uninstalling the app deletes all of it.

Advertising

The app displays ads served by Google AdMob (a small banner on the home screen, and an occasional full-screen ad after finishing some chapters) to help support the app's development. Ads only appear when your device is connected to the internet — none of the Bible reading, study, or listening features require ads or a connection to work.

To deliver and measure ads, Google AdMob and its partners may collect information such as your advertising ID, device information, and general (non-precise) location, and may use this information to personalize the ads you see. You can review or reset your advertising ID, or opt out of personalized ads, in your device's Settings app under Google → Ads. For details on how Google handles this data, see Google's Partner Sites policy and Google's Privacy Policy.

Notifications

If you enable the optional daily reading reminder, the app schedules a local notification directly on your device using Android's built-in notification system. This requires no external server and sends no data anywhere.

Children's Privacy

The app itself collects no data from anyone, including children. It does not knowingly direct advertising specifically at children, and ad requests for this app are configured to follow Google's guidance for apps with a general, mixed-age audience.

Changes to This Policy

If a future version of the app changes how data is collected — for example, adding new ad formats or analytics — this policy will be updated first, and the revised version will be posted at this same address.
