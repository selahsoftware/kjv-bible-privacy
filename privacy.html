Privacy Policy

Offline Holy Bible KJV · Effective July 18, 2026

Offline Holy Bible KJV does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Everything you do in the app — reading history, bookmarks, reminder settings — stays on your device.

Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, and the app does not transmit over the internet, any of the following:

  • Your name, email, or any account information — the app has no accounts
  • Analytics or usage tracking
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Location data
  • Contacts, photos, or any other device data

The app requires no internet connection to function, because the entire King James Bible text is bundled inside the app itself.

Data Stored On Your Device

The app stores the following data locally, only on your device:

  • Reading progress — which chapters you've marked as read
  • Bookmarks — verses you've saved
  • Last read location — so "Continue Reading" can pick up where you left off
  • Reminder settings — whether a daily reading reminder is enabled and at what time

None of this data ever leaves your device. Uninstalling the app deletes all of it.

Notifications

If you enable the optional daily reading reminder, the app schedules a local notification directly on your device using Android's built-in notification system. This requires no external server and sends no data anywhere.

Children's Privacy

Because the app collects no data from anyone, it does not collect data from children either.

Changes to This Policy

If a future version of the app adds a feature that involves data collection — for example, advertising or analytics — this policy will be updated first, and the revised version will be posted at this same address.
